**September 17 - December 3, 2025** | Grand Canyon University & Online Zoom  
ðŸ“ *Upcoming* | Every Wednesday, 5:30 PM - 7:00 PM

![TISU Logo](/static/images/tisu.jpg)

**TISU (Threat Intelligence Support Unit) training** is a collaborative program presented by **Grand Canyon University (GCU)** and **Arizona Cyber Threat Response Alliance (ACTRA)**, led by **Mike Manrod** (CISO) and our cybersecurity community.

**Program Overview:**
- **Free cybersecurity training** spanning one semester
- **Practical skills** based on MITRE ATT&CK and CIS18 frameworks
- **Career development** with pathways to Security Operations roles
- **Community partnership** creating volunteer opportunities

**Training Topics Include:**
- Basics of attack and defense
- Attack surface mapping
- Adversary profiling  
- Security operations fundamentals
- Hands-on labs and exercises

**Attendance Options:**
- **In-person**: GCU Campus, Building 57 (Cyber Center of Excellence)
- **Online**: Zoom available for remote participants
- **Open to all skill levels** - no prerequisites required

**Instructors Include:**
- Mike Manrod (CISO) - [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/manrod/) | [Twitter](https://twitter.com/CroodSolutions/)
- Dr. Pam Rowland - [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rowlandp/)
- Ezra Woods (Security Engineer) - [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ezrawoods/) | [X/Twitter](https://x.com/Shammahwoods)
- Cyrus Duncan (SOC Manager)
- Many community presenters and guest speakers

**Program Partners:**
- **Grand Canyon University (GCU)**: [gcu.edu](https://www.gcu.edu)
- **Arizona Cyber Threat Response Alliance (ACTRA)**: [actraaz.org](https://www.actraaz.org)
- **Community**: Led by [Mike Manrod](https://www.linkedin.com/in/manrod/) and local cybersecurity professionals

**Perfect for:**
- Students seeking cybersecurity career pathways
- Professionals looking to upskill in security operations
- Community members interested in threat intelligence
- Anyone wanting to gain practical cybersecurity experience

### Security-Focused Meetups

#### DC480 - Arizona Security Group
- **When**: First Tuesday of each month
- **Where**: Location varies (check website)
- **Cost**: Free
- **Website**: [dc480.io](https://dc480.io)
- **Twitter**: [@DC480AZ](https://twitter.com/DC480AZ)
- **Description**: A group of hackers, infosec professionals, and learners sharing insights and expertise.

---

#### EVSec - East Valley Security
- **When**: First Thursday of each month (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM)
- **Where**: Beer Research Institute, 1641 S Stapley Dr, Mesa
- **Cost**: Free
- **Website**: [evsec.org](https://evsec.org)
- **Slack**: #evsec on CentralSec Slack
- **Description**: Security discussions over beer and lockpicking in a casual setting.

---

#### SouthWest CyberSecurity Forum
- **When**: Monthly (6:00 PM - 8:00 PM)

- **Cost**: Free
