Christian Taillon is a cybersecurity professional specializing in threat hunting, SIEM operations, and AI security. He serves as a **Security Engineer** at **[Grand Canyon Education](https://www.gce.com/)** and **Director of Threat Intelligence** at the **[Arizona Cyber Threat Response Alliance (ACTRA)](https://actraaz.org/)**.

With over a decade of experience in security architecture, threat detection, and incident response, Christian focuses on deploying and enhancing Detection, Prevention, and Response tools including SIEM, EDR, and NTA systems. He leads security efforts in vulnerability management, cloud security, and maintains an active threat hunting and intelligence program.

Christian is deeply involved in the threat intelligence community, frequently sharing knowledge through presentations and open-source contributions. He's created valuable resources like the [Log4Shell Hunt](https://github.com/christian-taillon/log4shell-hunting) and [Sunburst Hunt](https://github.com/christian-taillon/sunburst-hunting) repositories, and actively contributes to projects like [OpenWebUI](https://openwebui.com/u/christiant/).

He's a strong advocate for Linux as a primary workstation, open-source solutions, and threat intelligence sharing through Sigma, YARA, and Snort/Suricata rules.
